Why IT Ethics Matters to Me

Since technology affects privacy, security, fairness, and trust, I believe IT professionals should not only focus on making systems work. They should also think about how those systems affect real people. This is especially important in cybersecurity because mistakes can expose sensitive information or create risks for users and organizations.
